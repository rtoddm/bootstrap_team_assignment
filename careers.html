Tiny Horse is always on the lookout for innovative and creative minds who have a passion for entertainment and digital, and a curiosity and drive to discover what’s next.

Our Culture

We work hard and we play hard. Whether we’re testing out new craft beers made by our local clients, or winning a Cannes Lions for creative campaigns, we’ve built a culture where we can trust each other to deliver. We don’t hesitate in asking each other for help, so if you aren’t afraid to raise your hand and step out of your comfort zone, let’s talk. When we aren’t at the office, it’s not unlikely that we’re hitting up happy hours or singing terrible karaoke on a Saturday night because we genuinely like each other. We seek candidates who want to become part of our family. We’re self-starters. If micromanagement is your thing, then Tiny Horse probably isn’t for you. Our team feels confident in taking the initiative to get the job done. But we also value collaboration. We trust the brainpower of every one of our employees, no matter their role, as we believe the sum is greater than the parts. Check your ego at the door, she can’t sit with us. We respect and support each other so that brainstorms can lead to million-dollar idea$. And lastly, we never forget where we came from. We pride ourselves on our unique roots across entertainment, production, and marketing. We’re an eccentric and diverse bunch. Don’t be surprised if you catch a partner dressed in drag. That’s show biz, baby!

Benefits

We want you here for a long time, not just a good time. We are dedicated to growing and developing our people, and ultimately, creating what’s next for you. In order to do that, we believe our people need to be taken care of. As part of the team, you can expect:

  • Unlimited PTO
  • Work From Home Fridays
  • Full Benefits – Medical, Dental, Vision
  • The Latest Tech – MacBook, etc.
  • Wellness Incentives
  • Stocked Kitchen and Bar
  • Paid Maternity and Paternity Leave
  • Woof Woof (Dog-Friendly Office)
